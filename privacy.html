Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026.

1. Who we are

Ashbrook Sourcing is a B2B sourcing and supply chain advisory firm registered in Ohio and based in Columbus. For questions about this policy or about personal data we hold, contact info@ashbrooksourcing.com.

2. What we collect

We collect only the information needed to communicate with prospects, clients, suppliers, and candidates. This typically includes name, business email, company, phone number, and any message you submit through our contact form. When you visit this website we collect basic technical information (IP address, browser, pages visited) through standard server logs.

3. Why we collect it

We use the information you provide to reply to your inquiry, deliver services you have engaged us for, manage supplier and client relationships, meet our legal and tax obligations, and improve the operation of this website. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. How long we keep it

Inquiries from prospects are kept for up to two years from the last contact, then deleted. Client and supplier records are kept for the duration of the engagement and for the periods required by US tax and contract law afterwards. You can ask us to delete information about you at any time by writing to info@ashbrooksourcing.com.

5. Who we share it with

We share information only with service providers strictly required to operate our business (email hosting, accounting, IT) and only under written confidentiality terms. We do not transfer personal data outside the United States unless a client specifically requires it, in which case the transfer is documented in the engagement contract.

6. Your rights

You can ask us to confirm what data we hold about you, to correct it, or to delete it. Send the request to info@ashbrooksourcing.com with enough detail for us to identify you. We will reply within 30 days.

7. Cookies

This site uses only essential cookies required for the contact form and for basic session handling. No third-party analytics or advertising cookies are set.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL.
